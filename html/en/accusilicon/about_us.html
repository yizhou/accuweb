About Us

Established in year 2010, Accusilicon Inc. is the marketing leading company in ultra low phase noise oscillator, ASIC and module, and their test and measurement solutions. Our product range includes SPXO, VCXO, TCXO, OCXO, I2S Hub, General Purpose SDM Hub etc. We are currently seeking business partners in order to provide high quality audio for the end users all over the globe.
